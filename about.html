Sara Antablian started her career as an office manager for the Irsfeld, Irsfeld & Younger law firm over 30 years ago. There, she worked with attorneys who practiced all types of law; one of which being real estate law.

Taking what she learned from the firm, combined with her experience as a real estate professional, Sara decided to pursue her career in real estate full-time.

Sara is a respected member of Glendale Boards of Realtor, California Association of Realtors, and National Association of Realtors. She specializes in Glendale, Burbank, La Crescenta, La Canada-Flintridge and surrounding cities.

Proud Resident of Glendale since 1982.
